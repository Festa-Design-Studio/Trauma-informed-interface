Usage Guidelines

Usage:

  • For supplementary information such as form help text
  • For image descriptions and attributions
  • For metadata such as timestamps and reading time
  • For subtle warning or informational messages

Accessibility:

  • Maintain sufficient color contrast ratio (minimum 4.5:1)
  • Don't rely solely on color to convey meaning (use icons or patterns)
  • Captions should be programmatically associated with their related content

Trauma-informed Considerations:

  • Lighter font weight creates appropriate visual hierarchy
  • Subdued but readable color reduces visual noise
  • Clear association with primary content reduces confusion
  • Warning captions use gentle indicators rather than alarming colors
  • Consistent positioning creates predictability
  • Adequate spacing improves readability
  • Small but readable text size maintains accessibility
