Usage Guidelines

Usage:

  • For categorization of content (topics, themes, difficulty levels)
  • In card footers to provide additional context
  • For filtering mechanisms in search or listing views
  • To indicate related topics or areas of interest

Accessibility:

  • Ensure sufficient color contrast between text and background
  • Don't use color alone to convey meaning
  • Keep text size readable (minimum 12px)
  • Include icons when helpful for visual recognition

Trauma-informed Considerations:

  • Gentle, soft colors reduce visual stress
  • Rounded shapes feel less threatening than sharp corners
  • Consistent styling across the interface creates predictability
  • Subtle borders create clear visual boundaries
  • Generous padding improves readability and reduces visual stress
  • Tag groups help users find content that feels safe to them
