Usage Guidelines

Usage:

  • For indicating status of items (completed, in progress, etc.)
  • For categorizing content (courses, resources, etc.)
  • For highlighting important information (content warnings)
  • For indicating difficulty or intensity level

Variations:

  • Round badges for status indicators
  • Rounded rectangle badges for categories
  • Icon badges for enhanced meaning
  • Visual difficulty indicators

Accessibility:

  • Use sufficient color contrast (minimum 4.5:1 for normal text)
  • Don't rely solely on color to convey meaning
  • Keep text size readable (minimum 12px)
  • Ensure icons have appropriate alt text or aria-label

Trauma-informed considerations:

  • Status badges use gentle, non-alarming colors
  • Content warnings are visually distinct without being alarming
  • Icons reinforce meaning for better comprehension
  • Difficulty indicators use visual cues beyond just text
  • Consistent styling creates predictability
  • Rounded shapes create softer, less threatening appearance
  • Subtle border on certain badges creates clear boundaries
  • Generous padding improves readability and reduces visual stress
  • Limited color palette avoids overwhelming sensory experience
