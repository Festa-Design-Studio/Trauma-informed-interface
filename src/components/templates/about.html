About Dr. G's Lab

We're on a mission to transform digital experiences through trauma-informed design principles that create safer, more empowering interactions for all users.

Our Story

Dr. G's Lab was founded in 2020 by Dr. Grace Geller, a clinical psychologist who recognized a critical gap in how digital products were being designed. After years of working with trauma survivors who found many digital experiences overwhelming, triggering, or disempowering, Dr. Geller decided to bridge the worlds of mental health and design.

What began as a small consultancy has grown into a comprehensive educational platform and design firm dedicated to transforming how designers and organizations approach digital experiences. Today, Dr. G's Lab works with companies across healthcare, education, finance, and technology to create digital spaces that respect the needs of all users, including those with trauma histories.

Our team now includes clinical psychologists, UX researchers, designers, and educators, all committed to advancing trauma-informed approaches in the digital world.

Our Mission & Values

At Dr. G's Lab, we're guided by a clear mission and core values that inform everything we do.

Our Mission

To transform digital experiences by empowering designers and organizations with the knowledge, tools, and support to create trauma-informed products that foster safety, trust, and healing for all users.

Safety First

We prioritize psychological and emotional safety in all our work, recognizing that safe environments are fundamental to healing and positive engagement.

Empowerment

We believe in giving users and designers the tools, knowledge, and choices they need to feel confident and in control of their experiences.

Inclusion

We design for the full spectrum of human experiences, ensuring that our work is accessible and responsive to diverse needs and backgrounds.
