Frequently Asked Questions

How often will I receive the newsletter?

The newsletter is typically sent monthly, but you can choose your preferred frequency during signup. You can modify this preference anytime through the subscription management link in any email.

Can I customize the content I receive?

Yes, during signup you can select your content preferences. These preferences help us tailor the newsletter to your interests. You can update these preferences at any time.

How do I unsubscribe if I want to?

Every newsletter includes a clear, one-click unsubscribe link at the bottom of the email. You can unsubscribe at any time, and we respect your decision immediately with no questions asked.

What if I need the newsletter in a different format?

We offer both HTML and text-only versions of our newsletter. The text-only version provides the same valuable content in a simpler format with fewer visual elements. If you need another format, please contact our support team.

Email Newsletter Template Documentation

Usage

This template provides a trauma-informed newsletter subscription page with Mailchimp integration that gives users control over the content they receive.

Key Components

  • Mailchimp-compatible subscription form with content preferences
  • Clear value proposition explaining what subscribers receive
  • Preview of previous newsletter content
  • Format options (HTML vs. text) to accommodate sensory preferences
  • Frequency control to prevent overwhelm
  • Privacy reassurance section
  • FAQ section addressing common questions

Implementation Notes

  • Replace the form action URL with your actual Mailchimp list URL
  • Update the hidden field name "b_XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX_XXXXXXXXXX" with your actual Mailchimp values
  • Modify group IDs (e.g., "group[58941][1]") to match your Mailchimp interest groups
  • Replace placeholder images with actual newsletter previews
  • Update testimonial content with real user feedback

Trauma-Informed Considerations

  • Optional fields clearly marked to reduce pressure
  • Multiple content format choices to accommodate different needs
  • Frequency control to prevent information overwhelm
  • Clear explanations of what to expect for predictability
  • Transparent privacy information to build trust
  • Multiple reminders about easy unsubscribe options
  • Emergency exit option for immediate departure if needed
  • FAQ section addresses anxiety-inducing uncertainties
