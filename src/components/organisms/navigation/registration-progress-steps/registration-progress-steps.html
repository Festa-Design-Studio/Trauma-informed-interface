Component Documentation

Usage Guidelines

  • Use in multi-step processes such as course registration, account creation, or checkout flows
  • Always maintain clear visual indication of completed, current, and upcoming steps
  • Provide descriptive labels that give users context about what happens at each step
  • For mobile devices, stack the steps vertically with connecting lines for better readability
  • Include optional subtitles on desktop to provide additional context about each step

Trauma-Informed Considerations

  • Provides clear indication of progress to reduce uncertainty and anxiety
  • Uses non-judgmental colors and language that focuses on progress rather than completion status
  • Avoids time-based indicators that might create pressure or stress
  • Maintains consistent visual representation across steps to improve predictability
  • Includes connecting lines between steps to show the relationship between stages
  • Uses adequate spacing to prevent overwhelming visual density
