Introduction to Trauma-Informed Design

4 hours Beginner

This foundational course introduces trauma-informed design principles and their importance in creating safe, inclusive, and empowering user experiences.

Self-paced Certificate

This course includes discussions of trauma and its effects. Content warnings are provided before sensitive material.

Course Overview

Gain a foundational understanding of trauma-informed design, why it matters, and how to apply its principles to create safer, more inclusive digital experiences. This course is suitable for designers, developers, and anyone interested in user-centered, compassionate design.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the core principles of trauma-informed design
  • Recognize the impact of trauma on user experience
  • Identify potential triggers in digital products
  • Apply trauma-informed strategies to your own work

Trauma-Informed Considerations

  • Clear content warnings and supportive messaging
  • Non-pressuring, self-paced learning environment
  • Consistent visual progress indicators
  • Accessible, inclusive design for all learners
  • Encouragement to take breaks and seek support

Usage

This page provides a detailed overview of a course, including its objectives, modules, trauma-informed considerations, and actions for enrollment. It is designed to be the standard reusable template for all course detail pages.

Key Components

  • Emergency exit button (always accessible)
  • Header with navigation and branding
  • Course title, metadata, and description
  • Content warning banner for sensitive material
  • Course overview, objectives, and trauma-informed considerations
  • Module list with status indicators
  • Clear call-to-action for enrollment or starting the course
  • Footer with support and accessibility links

Trauma-Informed Considerations

  • Clear, non-judgmental progress/status indicators
  • Supportive, non-pressuring language throughout
  • Content warnings and encouragement to take breaks
  • Accessible, inclusive layout and color palette
  • Consistent navigation and emergency exit for user safety
